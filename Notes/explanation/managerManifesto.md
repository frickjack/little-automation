# TL;DR

What does a manager do?

## Overview

* Look out for team
    - Insulate from external pressures
    - Give credit
    - Remove blockers
* Set goals
    - Team goals
    - Individual goals
* Track performance
    - Metrics
    - Feedback
    - Weekly meetings
* Interact with clients
    - Metrics
    - Communicate progress toward goals
    - Take on work/responsibility
